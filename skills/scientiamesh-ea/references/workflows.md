# Executive Assistant Workflows

## Meeting Prep

1. Search ScientiaMesh for the meeting topic, attendees, project, and open
   decisions.
2. Summarize only material that is returned by the mesh or supplied by the user.
3. Separate known facts from proposed next steps.
4. Capture new decisions after the meeting with source snippets and owners.

## Follow-up Tracking

1. Capture commitments with the owner, due date, source, and desired outcome.
2. Use `smesh search` before reporting whether a follow-up is new or already
   tracked.
3. Prefer short, actionable summaries over full transcript duplication.

## Personal Operating Context

Use durable captures for stable preferences, working norms, recurring projects,
and accepted delegation patterns. Avoid capturing temporary conversation filler
unless it changes a decision or creates a future obligation.
